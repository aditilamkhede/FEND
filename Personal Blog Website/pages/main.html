Ada Lovelace

Ada Lovelace was an English mathematician and writer. Her algorithm — which history has come to know as the first one designed for a machine to carry out — was intended to be used for Charles Babbage’s Analytical Engine. She is, therefore, credited as being the very first computer programmer. read more
